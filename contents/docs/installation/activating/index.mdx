## Getting Your Activation Key

Follow these steps to obtain your Olly activation key:

1. **Purchase the Activation Key**
   - Visit the [Olly pricing page](https://www.olly.social/#pricing) to purchase your activation key.
   - After purchase, you'll receive an email containing your activation key.

2. **For AppSumo Customers**
   - If you purchased through AppSumo, check your email for the activation key.

Important Note
    Keep your activation key safe and do not share it with others. Each key is tied to a single Olly account.

## Activating Olly

You have two options to activate Olly using your key:

<Tabs defaultValue="extension">
  <TabsList>
    <TabsTrigger value="extension">Via Browser Extension</TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="dashboard">Via Olly Dashboard</TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>
  <TabsContent value="extension">
    1. Click the Olly icon in your browser's navigation bar.
    2. Look for the "Add Activation Key" section.
    3. Enter your activation key in the provided field.
  </TabsContent>
  <TabsContent value="dashboard">
    1. Go to the [Olly Dashboard](https://www.olly.social/dashboard).
    2. Find the "Your Licences" section.
    3. Paste your activation key in the designated area.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during activation:

- Double-check that you've entered the key correctly.
- Ensure you're logged into the correct account.
- Clear your browser cache and try again.
