## What Are Custom Actions?
The Custom Actions feature in Olly v2.0 allows users to create personalized workflows by defining their own prompts and actions, such as generating stories or summaries. This update enhances flexibility, enabling users to tailor Olly's capabilities to their specific needs. Additionally, users can edit these actions over time, making it easier to refine their interactions. This feature is particularly beneficial for agencies and enterprises, as it facilitates the creation of AI personalities that can generate customized comments from various expert perspectives.

## Feature Benefits
* **Personalization**: Enables tailored interactions based on specific prompts, enhancing brand identity
* **Streamlined Management**: Simplifies social media management by creating customized workflows
* **Multi-Platform Integration**: Supports engagement across various social media platforms for consistent messaging
* **Improved Audience Engagement**: Facilitates more relevant and impactful interactions, driving user engagement
* **Flexible Editing**: Refine and update your custom actions over time to optimize performance
* **AI Personalities**: Create expert personas for diverse engagement strategies

## How To Create Custom Actions

### Step 1: Open the Olly Extension
Launch the Olly extension from your browser toolbar by clicking on the Olly icon in your Chrome extensions.

### Step 2: Go to Settings
Click on the gear icon or select Settings within the extension interface.

![Settings Icon](/images/features/custom-actions/custom-action-step1.png)

### Step 3: Navigate to Custom Actions
In the sidebar, go to the Custom Actions section.

![Custom Actions Menu](/images/features/custom-actions/custom-action-step2.png)

### Step 4: Fill in Custom Action Details
Complete the following fields to create your custom action:

**Title**: Give your action a clear and descriptive name
* Examples: Post Analyst, Quiz Generator, Content Rewriter, Rap Generator, Story Creator

**Action Type**: Choose the type of action from the dropdown list
* Available types: comment, summary, virality checker
* Note: Action type will be auto-generated based on the action title

**Emoji**: Add a relevant emoji to help you quickly identify the action
* The emoji will appear as an indicator (‚ò∫Ô∏è) when using the action

**Prompt**: Write the instruction or idea for what the action should do
* Examples:
  - "Generate a funny rap about AI"
  - "Turn this post into a quiz"
  - "Analyze this post for virality potential"
  - "Rewrite this content in a professional tone"

![Custom Action Configuration](/images/features/custom-actions/custom-action-step3.png)

### Step 5: Save Your Custom Action
Once all fields are filled in, click Save to store your custom action.

![Save Custom Action](/images/features/custom-actions/custom-action-step4.png)

## Using Custom Actions

### Step 6: Post Selection Process

1. **Refresh the webpage** where you want to use your custom action

2. **Locate the post** you want to analyze or engage with

3. **Select the text content**: Click and drag over the post's caption or text and ensure the desired content is fully highlighted

![Select Post Content](https://docs.olly.social/images/features/custom-actions/post-selection.png)

4. **Wait for Olly's popup window** to appear after highlighting the text

5. **Look for the emoji indicators** (‚ò∫Ô∏è) in the popup window that correspond to your custom actions

6. **Click the corresponding emoji** for your custom action

![Olly Popup with Custom Actions](https://docs.olly.social/images/features/custom-actions/checking-analysis.png)

### Step 7: Review Generated Output

1. Allow Olly to process the content based on your custom action prompt

2. Review the generated analysis or output in the popup window

3. Use, edit, or save the generated content as needed


## Editing Custom Actions

You can edit your custom actions at any time to refine their performance:

1. Return to Settings in the Olly extension
2. Navigate to Custom Actions
3. Select the action you want to edit
4. Update the Title, Action Type, Emoji, or Prompt as needed
5. Click Save to apply your changes

## Best Practices

* **Be Specific**: Write clear and detailed prompts to get the best results from your custom actions
* **Use Descriptive Titles**: Choose titles that make it easy to identify the action's purpose at a glance
* **Select Relevant Emojis**: Pick emojis that visually represent the action for quick identification
* **Test and Refine**: Experiment with different prompts and edit them based on the results
* **Create Multiple Variants**: Build several versions of similar actions for different use cases or tones
* **Organize by Purpose**: Group related actions together for easier workflow management

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

* **Custom action not appearing**: Refresh the webpage after creating a new action
* **Emoji not showing**: Clear your browser cache and reload the extension
* **Action not generating output**: Ensure your prompt is clear and specific enough for the AI to process
* **Text selection issues**: Make sure you've fully highlighted the desired content before the popup appears
* **Output quality concerns**: Refine your prompt with more detailed instructions or examples
* **Multiple actions confusion**: Use distinct emojis and clear titles to differentiate between actions
