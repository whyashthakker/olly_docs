## Troubleshooting
If you encounter any issues:
- **Posts not publishing:** Check that your social media accounts are still properly connected and authorized
- **Integration errors:** Reconnect your social media accounts through the Integrations panel
- **Enhancement not working:** Ensure you have an active internet connection and sufficient plan quota
- **Images not uploading:** Verify image format (JPG, PNG) and size limits (under 5MB recommended)
- **Calendar not loading:** Clear browser cache, refresh the page, or try a different browser
- **Schedule conflicts:** Check for duplicate posts scheduled at the same time
- **Ideas not generating:** Verify you've added at least 5 keywords and selected a category
For additional support or feature requests, please contact support at **support@olly.social** or visit our [Help Center](/docs/introduction).

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How many posts can I schedule in advance?
There is no limit to the number of posts you can schedule. However, platform-specific posting limits may apply.
### Can I schedule posts to multiple platforms simultaneously?
Yes, when creating a post, you can select multiple connected platforms to publish to all of them at once.
### What happens if my social media connection expires?
You'll receive a notification to reconnect your account. Scheduled posts will be paused until reconnection is complete.
### Can I edit a post after it's published?
Once published, you cannot edit through Olly. However, you can edit directly on the platform or delete and repost.
### How accurate are the viral idea engagement scores?
Engagement scores are AI-predicted based on historical performance data and current trends. Actual results may vary.
### Can I export my calendar or post data?
Yes, you can export your content calendar and analytics data from the Settings menu.
