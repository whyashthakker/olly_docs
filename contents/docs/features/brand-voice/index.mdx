## What is Brand Voice?

The Brand Voice feature is one of Olly.social's powerful tools, designed to enhance the quality of generated responses, making them more realistic and contextually relevant to your personal or professional brand. It allows users to create a personalized style for AI-generated comments and responses, ensuring consistency across all platforms and strengthening your brand identity.

## Feature Overview
The Brand Voice feature works as a smart profile creator within Olly that:
* Builds a comprehensive brand identity profile based on your inputs
* Ensures all AI-generated content aligns with your brand personality
* Enhances the quality and authenticity of all Olly-generated responses
* Creates consistency across all social media platforms and interactions
* Complements Olly's other social media management features

## How to Create Brand Voice

### Step 1: Open the Olly Extension
**Launch the Olly extension from your browser toolbar.**

![Open Extension](/images/features/brand-voice/open-extension.png)

### Step 2: Go to Settings
**Click on the gear icon or "Settings" within the extension interface.**

![Access Settings](/images/features/brand-voice/access-settings.png)

### Step 3: Navigate to Olly Configuration
**Inside Settings, select the Olly Configuration section.**

![Olly Configuration](/images/features/brand-voice/olly-configuration.png)

### Step 4: Select "Configure Brand Voice"
**In the Brand Voice section, click on Configure Brand Voice to begin setup.**

![Configure Brand Voice](/images/features/brand-voice/configure-brand-voice.png)

### Step 5: Fill in Brand Voice Details
**Complete all the required fields: Brand Name, Industry, Voice, Personality, Target Audience, Products/Services, USPs, and Mission. These help us generate an authentic and personalized brand voice for your AI interactions.**

![Fill Brand Details](/images/features/brand-voice/fill-brand-details.png)

### Step 6: Generate & Save Summary
**After filling in your information, click on Generate Summary, then hit Save to lock in your profile.**

![Generate Summary](/images/features/brand-voice/generate-summary.png)

### Step 7: Fetch Brand Voice in Extension
**Return to Olly Configuration in the extension settings and click Fetch Brand Voice to load your saved profile.**

![Fetch Brand Voice](/images/features/brand-voice/fetch-brand-voice.png)

## Benefits

* **Consistent Brand Identity** - Ensures all AI-generated content maintains your unique brand voice
* **Time-Saving** - Eliminates the need to manually adjust each response to match your brand tone
* **Increased Authenticity** - Makes AI-generated content indistinguishable from content written by you
* **Scalable Messaging** - Maintains brand consistency even when managing multiple accounts or high volumes
* **Enhanced Customer Experience** - Creates more personal, on-brand interactions with your audience

## Credit System

* **Credit Usage** - Generating a Brand Voice summary consumes 5 credits
* **Free Credits** - Users get 20 free credits upon signup (allowing 4 summaries)
* **Additional Credits** - Can be purchased at the rate of 100 credits for $1
* **Integration** - Works seamlessly with Olly API and OpenAI integration

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues with the Brand Voice feature:

* Make sure your Chrome browser is up to date
* Try disabling other extensions temporarily to check for conflicts
* Clear your browser cache and cookies, then restart Chrome
* Verify your internet connection is stable
* Contact support for persistent issues
