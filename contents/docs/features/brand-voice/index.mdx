## What is Brand Voice?
Brand Voice is a feature that enhances the quality of generated responses, making them more realistic and contextually relevant to your personal or professional brand. It allows users to create a personalized style for AI-generated comments and responses.

## How to Create Brand Voice


### Step 1: Access Brand Voice Section
* Navigate to the Brand Voice section in Olly and go to Configuration Brand Voice
![Brand Voice Access](/images/features/brand-voice/access.png)

### Step 2: Enter Personal & Professional Details
* Provide details related to your personal or professional brand to customize responses
![Personal Details](/images/features/brand-voice/personal-details.png)

### Step 3: Add Professional Information
* Enhance your Brand Voice by adding Professional Experience, Areas of Expertise, Industry Insights, and your Primary Industry
* This ensures AI-generated responses align with your career, skills, and industry trends for more relevant and personalized interactions
![Professional Info](/images/features/brand-voice/professional-info.png)

### Step 4: Add Brand Information
* Define your brand identity and strategy to ensure AI-generated responses reflect your brand's personality and positioning
* Include Core Brand Identity (Name, Personality, Voice, Values), Market Position (Products/Services, Unique Selling Points), and Strategy & Vision (Unique Approach, Content Topics, Content Strategy) for a consistent and authentic brand presence
![Brand Info](/images/features/brand-voice/brand-info.png)

### Step 5: Generate Summary
* After filling in all the details, click on "Generate Summary" before exiting
* This will create a high-level summary of your profile based on the provided information, ensuring AI-generated responses are more precise, relevant, and aligned with your personal or professional brand
![Generate Summary](/images/features/brand-voice/generate-summary.png)

### Step 6: Fetch & Enable Brand Voice
* After generating the summary, go to the Olly extension and navigate to Brand Voice
* Click on "Fetch Brand Voice" to pull in the latest summary
* Finally, enable the Brand Voice feature to apply it to your AI-generated responses
![Enable Brand Voice](/images/features/brand-voice/enable.png)

## Credit System

### Uses Credits
* Generating a Brand Voice summary consumes 5 credits
* Users get 20 free credits upon signup (allowing 4 summaries)
* Additional credits can be purchased (100 credits for $1)

### Improves AI Responses
* The AI uses your Brand Voice summary to generate more natural, human-like comments
* Works with Olly API and OpenAI integration

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during installation:
* Make sure your Chrome browser is up to date
* Try disabling other extensions temporarily to check for conflicts
* Clear your browser cache and cookies, then restart Chrome
