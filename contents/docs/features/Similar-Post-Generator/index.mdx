## What is Similar Post Generator?


The Similar Post Generator is one of Olly.social's powerful features, designed to help users create new social media posts that match the tone, structure, and style of existing ones. It works through Olly's Chrome extension, providing instant AI-generated content while you browse social media platforms.

## Feature Overview
The Similar Post Generator works as a smart content creation tool within Olly that:
* Provides AI-generated content through the Chrome extension
* Creates new posts inspired by existing content while browsing social media
* Delivers quick results with a simple highlight-and-generate process
* Leverages top AI models including GPT-4o, Claude 3, and Gemini
* Supports local models via Ollama for flexibility and privacy

## How to Use Olly's "Similar Post Generator"

### Step 1: Access Social Media
1. Open your preferred social media platform (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, or Facebook)
2. Ensure Olly's Chrome extension is active
3. Verify the extension icon in your toolbar
4. Browse through your feed



![Access Social Media](/images/features/similar-post-generator/access-social-media.png)

### Step 2: Select Post Content
1. Find the post you want to use as inspiration
2. Click and highlight the post's text content
3. Wait for Olly's popup window to appear

![Select Post Content](/images/features/similar-post-generator/select-post.png)

### Step 3: Look for the Olly popup
1. A small popup will automatically appear on the right side of your screen
2. Verify that the Olly interface is visible
3. Prepare to select the Similar Post Generator feature

![Olly Popup](/images/features/similar-post-generator/olly-popup.png)
