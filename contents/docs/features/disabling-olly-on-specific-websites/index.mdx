## What is Disabling Olly on Specific Websites?

## Who Will This Feature Help?

### 1. Social Media Managers
* If you manage multiple accounts across different platforms, this feature allows you to disable Olly on specific sites where you don't need it, ensuring a clutter-free workspace and smoother workflow.

### 2. Marketers & Content Creators
* Whether you're running ad campaigns or crafting engaging content, you can focus on the platforms that matter most while keeping Olly disabled on sites where you don't need automated suggestions.

### 3. Business Professionals
* If you use Olly for social engagement but want to keep it disabled on work-related websites, this feature helps you maintain a distraction-free professional environment.

### 4. Casual Users
* If you only want to use Olly on specific platforms, you can easily customize its presence and keep your browsing experience clean and personalized.

### Step 1: Access Olly Extension
* Open the Olly Chrome Extension – Click on the Olly icon in your browser toolbar to access the extension.

### Step 2: Navigate to Configuration
* Go to "Olly Configuration" – Navigate to the settings section where you can customize your Olly experience.
![Configuration](/images/features/disable-olly/configuration.png)

### Step 3: Find the Disable Option
* Scroll Down to "Disable Olly On" – Find this option in the settings to control where Olly appears.
![Disable Option](/images/features/disable-olly/disable-option.png)

### Step 4: Add Websites
* Add Website URLs – Simply enter the URLs of the websites where you want to manage Olly presence.

### Step 5: Save Your Changes
* Save Your Settings – Once added, Olly will automatically remain disabled on those websites until you change the settings.

## Feature Benefits

* **Focused Browsing**: Keep your browsing experience clean and focused
* **Enhanced Privacy**: Control where Olly is active for better privacy management
* **Improved Performance**: Optimize browser performance by limiting extension activity
* **Personalized Experience**: Customize Olly's presence based on your specific needs
* **Distraction Management**: Maintain productivity by limiting AI suggestions on certain sites

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during installation:
* Make sure your Chrome browser is up to date.
* Try disabling other extensions temporarily to check for conflicts.
* Clear your browser cache and cookies, then restart Chrome.
