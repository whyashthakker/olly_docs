## What is Reply to Comment?

The Reply to Comment feature is one of Olly.social's powerful tools, designed to help users respond to comments on their social media posts quickly and in a consistent, authentic tone. Using AI, it generates smart, context-aware replies that match your voice—whether the comment is a compliment, question, or critique. This saves time, boosts engagement, and ensures your interactions remain on-brand.

## Feature Overview
The Reply to Comment feature works as a smart response generator within Olly that:
* Provides instant reply suggestions through the Chrome extension
* Generates context-aware responses while you browse social media
* Delivers quick responses with a simple highlight-and-generate process
* Ensures replies match your brand voice and communication style
* Complements Olly's other social media management features

## How to Use Olly's "Reply to Comment"

### Step 1: Go to any social media platform
1. Open LinkedIn, Twitter (X), Instagram, or Facebook in your browser
2. Ensure Olly's Chrome extension is active
3. Navigate to a post with comments
4. Find comments you want to respond to

![Access Social Media](/images/features/reply-to-comments/access-social-media.png)

### Step 2: Highlight the text of a comment
1. Select the comment you want to generate a response for
2. Click and drag to highlight the text
3. Ensure the entire comment is selected

![Select Comment](/images/features/reply-to-comments/select-comment.png)

### Step 3: Look for the Olly popup
1. A small popup will automatically appear on the right side of your screen
2. Verify that the Olly interface is visible
3. Prepare to select the Reply to Comment feature

![Olly Popup](/images/features/reply-to-comments/olly-popup.png)

### Step 4: Click the icon
1. Open the popup menu
2. Tap on the reply icon (↩️) to activate the feature
3. Wait briefly for AI generation to begin

![Click Reply Icon](/images/features/reply-to-comments/reply-icon.png)

### Step 5: Get your reply to the comment
1. Review the instantly generated response
2. Copy the reply to use on your platform
3. Post the response or customize it as needed
4. Engage with your audience efficiently

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues with the Reply to Comment feature:

* Make sure your Chrome browser is up to date
* Try disabling other extensions temporarily to check for conflicts
* Clear your browser cache and cookies, then restart Chrome
* Verify your internet connection is stable
* Contact support for persistent issues
